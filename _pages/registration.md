### Travel support: 

- A limited amount of travel support is available to students based at Canadian universities. You can apply for this travel support during registration. You will need to upload a single pdf document containing the following: one-page cover letter describing your need for travel support, your CV, a copy of your student ID. 

- Travel support may become available for early-career researchers based at US institutes. You can apply for this potential travel support during registration. You will need to upload a single pdf document containing the following: one-page cover letter describing your need for travel support, your CV. For this purpose, early-career researchers include PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, pre-tenure faculty (e.g., assistant professors), and individuals within approximately 7 years of receiving their PhD, with consideration for career interruptions.
